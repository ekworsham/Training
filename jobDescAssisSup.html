ASSISTANT SUPERVISOR

JOB TITLE:ProScapes of Atlanta Assistant Supervisor

Purpose:

Assist the Supervisor in managing properties and crew and assume the Supervisors responsibilities in their absence.

Essential Function:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member Job Description plus:
⚫Coordinate and assist with loading and unloading equipment onto the truck.
⚫Report any truck, equipment and site-related issues to the Supervisor.
⚫Maintain a reasonable level of hearing to diagnose power equipment issues and remain aware of oncoming traffic while working near streets and parkways.
⚫Ensure all equipment is operational before leaving the branch each day by:
  • Checking oil levels
  • Cleaning the air filter
  • Sharpening blades
  • Carrying two (2) extra edger blades, trimmer line, 2-cycle oil, and one (1) spare spark plug
  • Starting all equipment before departing from the branch

    Qualifications & Requirements:


    ⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member plus:
