SUPERVISOR

JOB TITLE:ProScapes of Atlanta Supervisor

Purpose:

The Landscape Maintenance Supervisor is responsible for overseeing and managing landscape maintenance operations for commercial and residential properties. This role ensures that all work is performed efficiently, safely, and to company standards while leading and supporting a team of landscape maintenance team members.

Essential Function:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member and Assistant Supervisor Job Description plus:

Supervisory Responsibilities:


⚫Lead, train, and motivate landscape crew members to complete scheduled work efficiently and to a high standard.
⚫Assign daily tasks and ensure all crew members understand job expectations.
⚫Monitor crew member performance and provide coaching, guidance, and corrective action when necessary.
⚫Conduct and participate in regular safety meetings and ensure compliance with all company and OSHA safety regulations.

Operational Responsibilities:


⚫Plan and schedule maintenance activities, including mowing, pruning, weeding, touch up mulching, fertilization, pest control, and irrigation system maintenance.
⚫Inspect job sites before and after work is completed to ensure quality standards are met.
⚫Ensure all tools, equipment, and vehicles are in proper working order and coordinate necessary maintenance or repairs.
⚫Maintain accurate records of work completed, materials used, and crew hours.
⚫Communicate with operations manager regarding job progress, issues, and any additional services needed.
⚫Ensure all work is performed according to budgets, in a timely and cost-effective manner while maintaining high customer satisfaction.

Client Relations:


⚫Act as the primary point of contact for the operations manager regarding maintenance concerns, service schedules, and special requests.
⚫Address client requests/inquiries and resolve any service-related issues professionally and efficiently.
⚫Conduct site walkthroughs with operations manager to ensure satisfaction and identify potential enhancement opportunities.

Qualifications & Requirements:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member and Assistant Supervisor plus:
⚫High school diploma or equivalent (associates or bachelors degree in horticulture/landscaping, or a related field is a plus).
⚫Minimum of 2-5 years of experience in landscape maintenance, with at least 1 year in a supervisor role.
⚫Pesticide license within one (1) year of promotion.
⚫Strong knowledge of horticulture, plant care, irrigation systems, and pest management a plus.
⚫Ability to operate and maintain landscaping equipment, including mowers, trimmers, blowers, and pruning tools.
⚫Excellent leadership, organizational, and communication skills
⚫Bilingual (English/Spanish) is a plus.

Supervises:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Members and Assistant Supervisor

Work Schedule:


⚫Hours: Approximately 40 hours per week, beginning at 7:00 a.m. and typically ending at 4:00 p.m., with a one (1) hour unpaid lunch break. Employees working less than six (6) hours per day will not have a lunch period deducted.
⚫Additional Requirements:
  • Occasional Saturday work may be required during peak seasons, inclement weather, or to make up for rain delays.
  • Overtime may be necessary during peak seasons, inclement weather, or for special projects.
