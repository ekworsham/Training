OPERATIONS MANAGER

JOB TITLE:ProScapes of Atlanta Operations Manager

Purpose:

The Landscape Maintenance Operations Manager is responsible for overseeing and managing all aspects of landscape maintenance operations, ensuring efficiency, quality, and profitability. This role involves supervising teams, coordinating schedules, managing client relationships, and ensuring compliance with safety and company standards.

Essential Function:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member, Assistant Supervisor and Supervisor Job Description plus:

Supervisory Responsibilities:


⚫• Plan, schedule, and oversee daily landscape maintenance operations for commercial and residential properties.
⚫• Ensure crews are assigned tasks efficiently and that all work is completed on time and to quality standards.
⚫• Implement operational training and procedures to enhance efficiency and service quality.
⚫• Monitor job progress, inspect completed work, and ensure client satisfaction.
⚫• Manage equipment maintenance and ensure all tools and vehicles are in proper working condition.

Team Leadership & Supervision:


⚫• Train and supervise landscape maintenance supervisors and team members.
⚫• Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide coaching, feedback, and disciplinary actions as needed.
⚫• Foster a positive and productive work environment that promotes teamwork and accountability.
⚫• Ensure all employees follow company policies, safety guidelines, and industry best practices.

Client Relations:


⚫• Serve as a point of contact for clients, addressing inquiries, resolving concerns, and ensuring exceptional customer service.
⚫• Conduct regular site visits and walkthroughs to assess job quality and identify opportunities for additional services.
⚫• Develop and maintain strong relationships with property managers, homeowners, and commercial clients.

Financial & Budget Management:


⚫• Monitor and manage budgets, labor costs, and job profitability.
⚫• Oversee billing, invoicing, and financial tracking for maintenance contracts.
⚫• Assist in enhancement pricing (up to $1,000), proposals, scheduling and billing for existing clients.
⚫• Assist in maintenance contracts for new clients. Identify opportunities, identify names, email addresses and phone numbers.
⚫• Identify cost-saving opportunities while maintaining service quality.

Safety & Compliance:


⚫• Ensure all work is performed in compliance with OSHA regulations and company safety policies.
⚫• Conduct safety meetings and training sessions for employees.
⚫• Maintain documentation of safety inspections, incidents, and corrective actions.

Qualifications & Requirements:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member, Assistant Supervisor and Supervisor plus:
⚫• 5+ years of experience in landscape maintenance, with at least 2 years in a managerial or supervisory role.
⚫• Experience managing teams and overseeing multiple job sites.
⚫• Ability to read and interpret landscape plans, contracts, and service agreements.
⚫• Strong organizational, leadership, and communication skills.
⚫• Proficiency in Microsoft Office, scheduling software, and QuickBooks (preferred).

Supervises:


⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Team Members, Assistant Supervisor and Supervisor

Work Schedule:


⚫Hours: Approximately 40 hours per week, beginning at 7:00 a.m. and typically ending at 4:00 p.m., with a one (1) hour unpaid lunch break. Employees working less than six (6) hours per day will not have a lunch period deducted.
⚫Additional Requirements:
  • Occasional Saturday work may be required during peak seasons, inclement weather, or to make up for rain delays.
  • Overtime may be necessary during peak seasons, inclement weather, or for special projects.
