TEAM MEMBER

JOB TITLE: ProScapes of Atlanta Team Member

Purpose:

To safely install and maintain grounds for commercial and residential accounts in a professional manner while meeting all quality and safety standards.

Essential Function:


⚫Work in various weather conditions, including extreme heat, cold, dry, or rainy environments.
⚫Participate in training programs as required.
⚫Maintain a reasonable level of vision to inspect plants for pests and operate power equipment safely and effectively.
⚫Maintain a reasonable level of hearing to diagnose power equipment issues and remain aware of oncoming traffic while working near streets and parkways.
⚫Lift, carry, and transport materials over variable terrain for up to eight (8) hours per day, including but not limited to:

Material/Weight

  • Pine Straw Bales - Up to 50 lbs (one in each hand)
  • Fertilizer - 50 lbs per bag
  • Pine Bark - 50 lbs per bag
  • Cypress Mulch - 50 lbs per bag
  • B&B Trees - Up to 75 lbs
  • Plants in Containers - Up to 75 lbs
  • Tarps with Plant Clippings - Up to 100 lbs
  • Water Hoses - 50 lbs

    • ⚫Learn to effectively use various hand tools, including shovels, rakes, shears, and pruners (tool types vary by division).
    ⚫Operate mechanized equipment for up to eight (8) hours per day, including but not limited to:
  • Riding Mowers
  • Push Mowers
  • Edgers
  • Pruners/Hedge Trimmers
  • Backpack Blowers
  • String Trimmers
  • Chainsaws
  • Tillers
  • Planting
  • Water Tank

    • ⚫Read and understand pesticide labels and learn proper mixing and application techniques.
    ⚫Tree pruning and related tasks.
    ⚫Arrive on time daily and participate in pre-shift meetings to discuss the work plan.
    ⚫Maintain a neat, clean, and organized work area.
    ⚫Assist in loading and unloading equipment, tools, and supplies onto trucks.
    ⚫Ensure all equipment is fully operational before departing from the shop or trailer each day.
    ⚫Report landscape problem areas to the supervisor.
    ⚫Perform all job-related tasks in a safe, efficient, and professional manner.
    ⚫Work collaboratively with team members, adhere to company policies and procedures, and demonstrate confidence in task execution.
    ⚫Clean and properly store used equipment daily.
    ⚫Maintain a professional appearance at all times and adhere to the company uniform policy.

    Qualifications & Requirements:


    ⚫High school diploma or equivalent.
    ⚫Ability to lift up to 50 lbs. and work in various weather conditions.

    Reports To:


    ⚫ProScapes of Atlanta Supervisor

    Work Schedule:


    ⚫Hours: Approximately 40 hours per week, beginning at 7:00 a.m. and typically ending at 4:00 p.m., with a one (1) hour unpaid lunch break. Employees working less than six (6) hours per day will not have a lunch period deducted.
    ⚫Additional Requirements:
  • Occasional Saturday work may be required during peak seasons, inclement weather, or to make up for rain delays.
  • Overtime may be necessary during peak seasons, inclement weather, or for special projects.
